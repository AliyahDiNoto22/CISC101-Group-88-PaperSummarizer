# Module 4: Rendering & Refinement

## Responsibilities
- **Final structure:** Assemble all outputs.
- **Formatting:** Ensure consistent headings, tables, and word limits.
- **Variants:** Generate expert and lay summaries.
- **Glossary & warnings:** Add mini-glossary and checks/warnings.

## Inputs
- **Section summaries:** From Section Loop.
- **Warnings:** From Guardrails.

## Outputs
- **Paper Summary:** ≤300 words.
- **Section-by-Section Table:** Ordered, ≤150 words per section.
- **Expert + Lay Summaries:** Two variants.
- **Mini-Glossary:** 5–10 terms.
- **Checks & Warnings:** Consolidated list.

## Steps
1. **Compile** per-section summaries in original order.
2. **Synthesize** unified summary ≤300 words.
3. **Render** expert and lay variants with consistent formatting.
4. **Insert** glossary and warnings; verify word limits.
