# Module 2: Section Loop

## Responsibilities
- **Iterate sections:** Summarize each section in ≤150 words.
- **Enforce constraints:** Maintain order, terminology, and logical flow.
- **Tailor audience:** Adjust language for expert vs lay readers.

## Inputs
- **Normalized sections:** From Intake & Setup.
- **Audience:** Expert or lay.

## Outputs
- **Per-section summaries:** Labeled, ordered, ≤150 words each.
- **Flags:** Missing/empty indicators per section.

## Steps
1. **For each section,** extract key aims, methods, results, and implications.
2. **Write summary** in ≤150 words using consistent terminology.
3. **Attach flags** for missing/empty or <50-word sections when applicable.
